## Benefits of New Name

### "Free Resume Maker" vs "ATS Resume Generator"

**Advantages**:
1. ✅ **"Free"** - Immediately communicates value (no cost)
2. ✅ **"Resume Maker"** - More common search term (higher volume)
3. ✅ **User-Friendly** - Less technical, more accessible
4. ✅ **Broader Appeal** - Not just ATS-focused
5. ✅ **Search Volume** - "free resume maker" has higher search volume
6. ✅ **Marketing** - Easier to promote (FREE is compelling)

**Still Includes**:
- ATS optimization (in subtitle and content)
- AI-powered (emphasized throughout)
- Professional quality (maintained)

## SEO Impact

### Keyword Research
- **"free resume maker"**: High search volume
